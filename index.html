About Me

I’m an emerging Data Analyst and recently completed the Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate. In this training, I learned how to aggregate and analyze data and present findings. I have been working hands-on with data tools, including spreadsheets, SQL, Tableau, and R programming. I am also presently pursuing my Master's degree in Human Services through Purdue Global University.

My interest in using data as a source of information began during my 11 years in banking operations, and I frequently used data analysis and customer service skills in the Child Welfare field. I have 6 years of experience working in Child Welfare where I was a case manager for 2 years, and was then promoted to unit supervisor. My most recent role in the Child Welfare Field was as the Conditions for Return Supervisor where I built a team of specialists, oversaw their work, analyzed data, and created tools to identify system deficits.

As a data analyst, I am passionate about using data to solve complex problems and am driven to solve issues that will better serve the community and those in need. Known for being a quick learner and team player with a commitment to producing high-quality work, I’m excited about the opportunity to contribute my skills and experience to any team or organization.
